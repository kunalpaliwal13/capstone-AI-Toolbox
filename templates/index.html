What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a way of making computers think and learn like humans. It powers things like smart assistants, self-driving cars, and recommendation systems. Instead of just following instructions, AI learns from data, finds patterns, and makes decisions. From chatbots to medical diagnosis, AI is shaping the future, and understanding it can open up a world of possibilities!
